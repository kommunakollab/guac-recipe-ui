THE GUACAMOLE

Ingredients:

  • 2–3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • 1–2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp cilantro (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • Mash the avocados
  • Add onion, tomato, cilantro
  • Mix in lime juice, salt, pepper
  • Stir until combined
  • Serve fresh
Serve it fresh, dip in, and let every bite hit like a flavor explosion. Enjoy!
